Our Story

Kaida Coffee and Bakery is your neighborhood sanctuary in the heart of NYC. We pride ourselves on the finest artisanal coffee and freshly baked pastries delivered with love.

"Crafting sweet moments on Ludlow Street since day one."

Every loaf and every cup is prepared with the same dedication to quality that our regulars have come to expect. We are more than just a café; we are a community.
