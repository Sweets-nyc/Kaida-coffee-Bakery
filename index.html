Handcrafted with Passion

Our Story

Welcome to Kaida Coffee and Bakery, your cozy haven in the heart of New York! Our café is dedicated to bringing you the finest coffee and freshest baked goods, crafted with love and passion.

At Kaida, we believe in using only the highest quality ingredients, ensuring every bite and sip is a delightful experience. Our menu features a variety of artisanal coffees, from rich espressos to creamy lattes, paired perfectly with our pastries, muffins, and bread.

We take pride in our warm and inviting atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for a relaxing break or a catch-up with friends. Join us at Kaida Coffee and Bakery, where every visit feels like a warm hug.

We look forward to serving you and becoming your favorite neighborhood café!
